# Propositional Logic Extraction

You are given a short context involving an agent.  
Extract the key facts and express them as simple logical propositions.
Answer only with the raw logical propositions as in the example.

## Example
Context: Anna is in the kitchen. Anna sees the apple on the table.  
Output:
- Proposition 1: Anna is located in the kitchen.
- Proposition 2: Anna sees the apple.
- Proposition 3: The apple is on the table.
